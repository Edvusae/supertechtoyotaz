How It All Began

15 Years. One
Mission.

Supertech Toyotaz was founded in 2009 by Emmanuel Choto, a Toyota-trained master technician with a dream of building Harare's finest specialist workshop. After years working at authorised dealerships and witnessing customers being overcharged for work they didn't need, Emmanuel set out to create something different — a workshop built on genuine expertise, honesty, and a true love for the Toyota brand.

Starting with just three bays and a small team of dedicated mechanics in Workington, word quickly spread that Supertech Toyotaz was the place to take your Toyota. Year after year, the workshop grew — expanding to 12 service bays, investing in cutting-edge diagnostic equipment, and building a team of 12 specialised technicians.

"We built this workshop for Toyota owners who refuse to settle. Your vehicle deserves specialists who understand every bolt, every sensor, every system — not a generalist working from a manual."
— Emmanuel Choto, Founder & Master Technician

Today, Supertech Toyotaz services everything from the humble Corolla to full Land Cruiser 300 series, commercial Hiace fleets, and the latest hybrid models. Our commitment to the Toyota ethos of reliability, durability, and value for money runs through everything we do.

Our Journey

Milestones &
Growth.

2009

The Beginning

Emmanuel Choto opens Supertech Toyotaz in a 3-bay workshop in Workington, Harare, with a team of 4. The vision: Harare's best Toyota specialist, built on honesty and expertise.

2012

First Expansion

Demand forces a major expansion. The workshop doubles to 6 service bays and the team grows to 7 technicians. First fleet accounts signed with two Harare-based logistics companies.

2015

Advanced Diagnostics

Major investment in Toyota-specific computerised diagnostic equipment — the first independent workshop in Harare to offer dealership-level diagnostic capability for all Toyota models.

2018

500th Fleet Vehicle

Supertech Toyotaz reaches a landmark milestone: servicing its 500th fleet vehicle. Fleet accounts now span logistics, mining, NGOs, and government departments across Zimbabwe.

2021

Hybrid Certification

Team certified in hybrid vehicle technology, enabling full service and repair of Toyota Prius, Yaris Cross Hybrid, and HiLux hybrid models — ahead of any other independent workshop in Harare.

2024

Full Workshop Renovation

Complete facility upgrade: 12 fully-equipped bays, new alignment and wheel balancing technology, customer lounge, and parts department. Supertech Toyotaz at its best.

Today

Harare's #1 Toyota Specialist

Over 4,800 vehicles serviced, 12 expert technicians, and a reputation built on 15 years of trust. Still family-owned, still passionate about every Toyota we touch.
